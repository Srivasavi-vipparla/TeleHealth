Acne

    Acne is a skin condition that occurs when your hair follicles become plugged with oil and dead skin cells.
    It causes whiteheads, blackheads or pimples. Acne is most common among teenagers, though it affects people of all ages.
    Effective acne treatments are available, but acne can be persistent.

Anxiety

    Anxiety is a condition of persistent and uncontrollable
    nervousness,
    stress,
    and worry
    that is triggered by anticipation of future events,
    memories of past events,
    or ruminations over day-to-day events,
    both trivial and major,
    with disproportionate fears of catastrophic consequences.

Depression

    Depression is a mood disorder that causes a persistent feeling of sadness and loss of interest.
    Also called major depressive disorder or clinical depression,
    it affects how you feel,
    think and behave and can lead to a variety of emotional and physical problems.

Diarrhea

    Diarrhea is described as an increase in the frequency of bowel movements
    or a decrease in the consistency of stools that causes the discharge of watery, loose stools.
    The severity of diarrhea is determined by the size
    and number of stools passed within a period of time.

Dizziness

    Dizziness is a term used to describe a range of sensations,
    such as feeling faint, woozy, weak or unsteady.
    Dizziness that creates the false sense that you or your surroundings are spinning or moving is called vertigo.
    Dizziness is one of the more common reasons adults visit their doctors.

Ear Infection

An ear infection (sometimes called acute otitis media) is an infection of the middle ear, the air-filled space behind the eardrum that contains the tiny vibrating bones of the ear.

Headache

    Headache is pain in any region of the head.
    Headaches may occur on one or both sides of the head,
    be isolated to a certain location,
    radiate across the head from one point,
    or have a viselike quality.
    A headache may appear as a sharp pain, a throbbing sensation or a dull ache.

Itchy Skin

    Itchy skin is an uncomfortable, irritating sensation that makes you want to scratch.
    Also known as pruritus (proo-RIE-tus), itchy skin is often caused by dry skin

Migraine

    Migraine headaches can cause throbbing in one particular area that can vary in intensity.
    Nausea and sensitivity to light and sound are also common symptoms.

Rash
